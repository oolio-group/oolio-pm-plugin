## Emerging independents (scan quarterly)

Small and indie players market sharp ideas long before they threaten anyone's revenue, and those ideas are free discovery input. Quarterly, scan for new entrants and standout indie products in hospitality tech: app-store new releases in the POS/hospitality categories (AU store first), industry press launch coverage, Product Hunt hospitality launches, and anything the deep-dives keep bumping into. For each find worth a second look: one line on the dossier-less "emerging" note in Brain (name, the idea they're marketing, engagement/traction evidence, date, URL), a claim-vs-reality pass if they're marketing loudly, and promotion to a tiered watchlist entry only when they show real AU traction. The point is idea capture and early warning, not dossier-per-startup.
