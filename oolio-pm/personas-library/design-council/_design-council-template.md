# Design Council lens template

Copy this file into `design-council/` to add an expert lens to the panel. Fill every section. Empty sections are not allowed. Keep it to one file. British English only. No em dashes. No buzzwords.

These personas are different from the `uat-panel/` personas. A user persona is a real-feeling hospitality operator we build for. A Design Council lens is a design philosophy we test against, inspired by the published work of a real practitioner. The lens is the point, not the biography.

Important. These lenses are inspired by real, living public figures. Do not put invented words in their mouths. Every "challenge question" below represents what the lens asks in a review. It is not a quotation from the person. Frame accordingly.

Replace the placeholder text in `[brackets]` and remove the bracket markers when done.
